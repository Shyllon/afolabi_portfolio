About Me

I am Afolabi Shyllon, a passionate leader, strategist, and developer who is committed to leveraging technology to build better futures and drive impact. With over 7 years of experience in various roles across school administration, media, communications, and project management, I have honed a broad set of skills that allows me to seamlessly combine leadership, technical expertise, and creativity.

My academic journey began with a National Certificate in Education (NCE) in English/Yoruba, which provided me with the pedagogical foundation to positively impact lives through education. This training allowed me to refine my teaching approach and laid the groundwork for my future leadership roles, equipping me to inspire and guide others effectively.

Building on this, I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in History and Diplomatic Studies and a Master’s degree in History and Strategic Studies, where I gained a deeper understanding of societal structures and strategies for development. This knowledge has been instrumental in my work across various sectors, where I focus on addressing educational and community challenges.

From serving as the Class Governor during my undergraduate days to leading impactful community-driven initiatives during my National Youth Service in Cross River State, I have always been driven by a desire to inspire and contribute meaningfully. I spearheaded projects such as the construction of a community roundabout and organized excursions to expose students to new possibilities, earning me a State Recognition Award for outstanding service.

In my career, I have demonstrated my leadership and innovative capabilities in several ways:

  • As the Team Lead at PrismPulse Media, I increased audience growth by 40% within six months by crafting targeted strategies and campaigns.
  • In my role at Cam Dairy Foods Limited, I improved operational efficiency by 15% as the Logistics Distribution & Maintenance Lead.
  • As the Executive Assistant/Administrator at Brook Stones Group of Schools, I successfully coordinated schedules and operations using tools like Calendly, Monday.com, and Canva.

Now, I am on a mission to blend my leadership abilities with my technical prowess as a full-stack developer. I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, AI/Machine Learning and SQL, and I have built model-driven and canvas apps using low-code platforms. I am particularly passionate about using technology to build better futures and foster greater access to opportunities, with a strong focus on women, youth, and environmental sustainability.

Outside of work, you will often find me reading, traveling, or playing board games. I am always excited to collaborate, explore new ideas, and push the boundaries of what is possible.
